Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  CopyPower Digital LLP

ScoutHook ("we", "our", "the Service") is operated by CopyPower Digital LLP. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. What we collect

  • LinkedIn sign-in data — when you sign in with LinkedIn we store your LinkedIn name and profile photo URL for display inside the app. We do not store your LinkedIn password.
  • Session cookie — we store a signed session cookie in your browser to keep you logged in. In review-mode, we do not persist LinkedIn OAuth access or refresh tokens in our database.
  • Voice profile — content niche, audience description, writing samples, and the AI-generated voice fingerprint you create inside the app.
  • Generated posts — posts you generate and save as drafts, along with quality scores.
  • Usage identifiers — a tenant ID and user ID used to scope your data within the platform.

2. How we use it

  • To generate LinkedIn posts tailored to your voice and audience.
  • To help you post on LinkedIn manually (for example by copying your draft to the clipboard and opening LinkedIn).
  • To show your LinkedIn profile name and photo inside the app UI.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third party for marketing purposes.

3. LinkedIn data

In review-mode, ScoutHook uses LinkedIn only for authentication (Sign In with LinkedIn / OpenID Connect). We use your LinkedIn profile name and photo only for display within the app.

We do not store data about other LinkedIn members. Data obtained via the LinkedIn API is not combined with data from other sources.

LinkedIn-sourced profile data (name, photo) is deleted when you disconnect your LinkedIn account.

4. Data retention

  • Generated posts and drafts are retained until you delete them.
  • Your signed session cookie remains in your browser until you disconnect or it expires.
  • Temporary generated files (PNG, ZIP, PDF visuals) are automatically deleted after 24 hours.

5. Your rights

You may request deletion of all your data at any time by disconnecting LinkedIn from the app and contacting us at the address below. We will delete all associated records within 30 days.

If you are in the EU/EEA you have additional rights under GDPR including access, rectification, portability, and the right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.

6. Security

Access tokens are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before being stored. We use HTTPS for all data in transit. Access to the platform is restricted by user-scoped identifiers.

7. Third-party services

  • LinkedIn API — used for OAuth authentication (Sign In with LinkedIn). Subject to LinkedIn's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude API — used to generate post content. Your writing samples and ideas are sent to Anthropic's API. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

8. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
